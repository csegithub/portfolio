Pursuing something you never thought you would pursue? Me too. My initial aspiration, since kindergarten, was to enter the medicine field. Because of my previous passions and a very special class, I aspire to be a Biotechnologist. As high school dawned upon me and my Computer Science and Engineering class became the center of my attention, my perspective started to change. CSE, or Computer Science and Engineering, is a class in which students learn different coding languages and are able to create our own websites, games, and applications from the scratch. Through group projects, I saw how much the machine we know as the computer has brought us together and helped humans to connect. I was able to experience firsthand, the power of a computer and its code.

As a freshman, I have been on the Junior Varsity Tennis Team and have been a Girl Scout for 5 years. I have also been volunteering on almost a weekly basis for the last couple of years through the Northern California Sai Organization. I have also won a few awards for my artwork through Reflections and Bighelp. Through all of these experiences I have connected with others, built bonds and bettered myself in one way or another. Whether the problem is losing a tennis match or being deprived of that last beautiful Girl Scout badge, having a positive attitude and being tolerant and focused has helped me achieve exactly what I wanted. I feel theses qualities will help me in the lab or in the workplace.

All these extracurriculars and daily duties have provided me with skills and have given me the characteristics that I have today. I consider myself to be self-motivated and ambitious because of tennis, cooperative yet independent because of Girl Scouts, creative from my art and photography, and understanding from volunteering. Lastly, because I am a CSE student, I have learned to be persistent and plan accurately. Everything I do is because I, myself, am motivated to do them. I excel in anything I set my mind to and my mind has been set on a Biotechnology major and it is a goal that I will definitely reach.

Thank you for taking the time to read about my accomplishments and aspirations.
